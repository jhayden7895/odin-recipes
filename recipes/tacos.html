Tacos

These classic Ground Beef Tacos feature homemade taco meat loaded into freshly fried taco shells. Serve up with all your favorite toppings for an easy family meal that takes less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Pepper
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Oil
  • Corn Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef until it is completely brown.
  2. Allow taco meat to simmer briefly in sauce for flavor.
  3. Fry corn tortillas in light oil.
  4. Fill taco shells with cooked beef.
